Lab 2 Part 2: Data Analysis and Dashboard Creation

Continuation: Now let's explore Snowsight's powerful visualization and filtering features, then create interactive dashboards.

2. Analyze Results and Data

2.1 Explore Query Details

  1. Review Query Details Pane: Click the Query Details pane to the right of your results
  2. Examine information like role, virtual warehouse, and execution statistics
  3. View Contextual Statistics: Scroll down to see statistics for YEAR and SUM_GROSS_REVENUE columns

üí° Contextual Statistics

Snowsight automatically generates interactive statistics for each column in your query results. These can be used as filters to explore your data dynamically.

2.2 Interactive Filtering

Filter by Year:

  1. Activate YEAR filter: Click on the YEAR filter in the contextual statistics pane
  2. Select single year: Click the leftmost column (2012) in the graph
  3. Select multiple years: Drag the right selector to include both 2012 and 2013
  4. Experiment: Try different bar combinations to see how data changes
  5. Clear filter: Click "Clear filter" at the top of the statistics pane
  6. Close filter: Click the X in the upper right corner

Filter by Revenue:

  1. Click SUM_GROSS_REVENUE filter: Observe the revenue distribution
  2. Test filtering: Click each bar and observe data changes
  3. Note gaps: Clicking between bars shows "Query produced no results"
  4. Clear and close: Use Clear filter then X to close

Check your understanding

  • How do contextual statistics help with data exploration?
  • Why might there be gaps in the revenue filter?
  • What insights can you gain from the year-over-year trend?
